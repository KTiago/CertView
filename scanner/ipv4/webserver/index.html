CertWatch Project

We are researchers in the field of computer security currently conducting internet wide scans to collect TLS certificates on port 443. The collected data is not used for any malicious purpose.

Our scan consists in a TCP SYN scan to discover hosts reponsive on port 443 on the entire IPv4 range followed by a TLS handshake to collect the certificates from reponsive hosts.

The scan is purposefully spread over time in order to limit any negative impact. The order of hosts is chosen randomly to evenly distribute the load, resulting in any /16 IPv4 range being subject to less than 1 TCP SYN packet (and TLS handshake) every 2 seconds.

If nonetheless you want your subnetwork to be excluded from future scan from our project, please send an email to abuse.certview@gmail.com with the list of subnetworks to be blacklisted and we will promptly honor your request.
